WELCOME TO Eattendo

"Eattendo Attendance" is a web-based attendance management system that provides a simple and efficient way to take attendance online.

The platform generates a unique secret key that can be shared with the students or attendees, which they can use to mark their attendance quickly and easily.

With Eattendo attendance, you can say goodbye to the hassle of manual attendance management and streamline the entire process with just a few clicks. Whether you're a teacher, a trainer, or an event organizer, Eattendo attendance is the perfect solution for all your attendance management needs.

The platform is user-friendly, secure, and customizable, offering a simple and efficient way to manage attendance online.
